Recommendation Score

Likelihood to recommend the LDS to a colleague (0–10)

How this is calculated: Each participant rated how likely they would be to recommend the LDS program on a 0–10 scale. Scores of 9–10 are "Recommend," 7–8 are "Neutral," and 0–6 are "Would Not Recommend." The score is calculated by subtracting the % who would not recommend from the % who would. It ranges from −100 to +100. Any positive score means more promoters than detractors. Above +20 is considered strong for a professional development program.
